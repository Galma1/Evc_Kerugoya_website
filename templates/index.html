About our Church

We Are Here to Help!

It is known that the major mission of one who is born from Christ is to share what has been done for him for others. Therefore, a church called 'Jesus Christ Proclaimers' (JCP), established in Finfinnee (Addis Ababa) around Gullele Paster, aimed to fulfill the mission of the Bible, preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom of God on all sides. These include various methods such as outreach preaching and internal service provisions.
